Undergraduate Program

About the Program

The Undergraduate Program in Information Systems at UMBC prepares students for careers in a fast-growing field with a strong foundation in technology, problem-solving, and business management. Students will gain expertise in areas such as software development, data analysis, and network administration.

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to equip students with the skills necessary to excel in the field of information systems. Students will complete coursework in areas such as database management, web development, business analytics, and project management.
